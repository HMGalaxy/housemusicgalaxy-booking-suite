# Galaxy Cue Business OS v9.0

This release deliberately optimizes the Business OS while keeping the Client App and Admin OS separated and stable.

## Navigation
Dashboard, Events, Clients, Calendar, Messages, Documents, Music Planner, Financials, Automation, Analytics and Settings. The Client Portal cross-app link has been removed from the Business sidebar.

## Product areas
- Dashboard 2.0
- Event Workspace
- Client Workspace
- Calendar and timeline
- Financial hub
- Automation foundation
- Design-system and responsive polish
