# Galaxy Cue v10.0.1

## Verified Business OS fixes
- Business Login opens the Business authentication modal.
- Consultation and planning template cards route directly to working workspaces.
- Horizontal workflow tabs and the workflow status card are removed from consultation/planning workspaces.
- Template Settings now supports protected built-in items and deletable custom items.
- Add Custom Item includes category, consultation/planning visibility, enabled state, equipment relevance, and notes.
- Custom deletion requires confirmation.
- Enabled template items appear dynamically in consultation and planning workspaces.
